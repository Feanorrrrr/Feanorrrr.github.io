About Me

I am an Electrical Engineering student who enjoys learning through hands-on projects. My academic interests include digital systems, embedded systems, and software tools that support engineering work.

Over time, I have become especially interested in the connection between hardware and software. I like working on projects that are practical, organized, and directly useful. These experiences have helped me build both technical skills and a clearer understanding of the kind of engineer I want to become.

I am currently focused on strengthening my foundation in engineering design, programming, and system-level thinking. In the future, I hope to continue developing projects that combine problem-solving, creativity, and real-world application.
